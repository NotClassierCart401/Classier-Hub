Your ship must follow these guidelines:
  • The helm/control seat must be named "Captain" and have the AI set to Ship Pilot
  • The ship must have 2 fuel tanks named diesel1 and diesel2 and a battery named battery1
  • There must be exactly 1 spot for a container with 4 electrical connectors at each end. The area should be a 11x29 (or just use the preset container)
  • W/S should control engine throttle, A/D should control steering, and 1 should be the engine starter
  • Any seats on the boat that NPCs should sit in must be named Seat
